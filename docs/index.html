What you will walk away with

Five things your team will take back to work

01

A shared language for AI across the organisation

Everyone leaves with the same framework. Decisions about when and how to use AI become easier to discuss and align on.

02

Practical skills that work from day one

Your team will know how to communicate with AI systems clearly, get useful results, and avoid the common mistakes that waste time.

03

Confidence to evaluate what AI produces

AI gets things wrong. Your team will know how to spot the issues, check what matters, and keep human judgement where it belongs.

04

An approach to AI that fits your values

You will leave with clear principles for using AI responsibly, in a way that reflects what your organisation stands for.

05

Skills that stay relevant when the next model comes out

This workshop is not built around specific tools. The 4D framework is deliberately designed to be model-agnostic. Whatever AI looks like in two years, your team will have the thinking skills to work with it well.
